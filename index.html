Crystal Collector

You will be give a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number above the crystals. You lose if your score goes above that number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, each crystal will be assigned a random number between 1-12.

Good Luck!
